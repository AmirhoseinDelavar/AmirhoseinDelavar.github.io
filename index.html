Yasin Delavar, Amirhossein Delavar, Dividing land into desired parcels concerning cut trees, fixed constructed buildings on the block, and the property area. Includes three stages: block road selection (divide-conquer method), parcel division (genetic algorithm), new building selection, and placement in each parcel (genetic algorithm). Block road selection divides blocks into halves concerning cut trees and size equality or access of each part to that new road. Parcel division splits each prior mini-block into a predicted number of parcels concerning cut trees and parcels area and count. Unique building selection and placement try to choose an appropriate-sized building and align it with the main road on the parcel concerning the feasibility of construction.
